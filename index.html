The taste

Made with love in the north of France since 1883, brewed with barley malt and hop extracts, its famous yeast A conceals the secrets of its taste so unique and identical throughout the world. Our iconic product is tequila flavored and has a sunny, clear and brilliant yellow colour. Bottom-fermented, brewed with barley malt, it has a yield of 5.9% vol.To be enjoyed chilled between 4°C and 6°C, it offers a powerful and pronounced taste in the mouth, with subtle lemony notes and a few spicy touches.

The passion for our job as a brewer pushes us to constantly innovate in order to offer beers with new flavours, brewed according to techniques that perpetuate local know-how and traditions. The company is the link between numerous local, regional and global brands and offers a unique, innovative and diversified range of products!

Special agreements

To hop our beers, we signed an agreement with Comptoir Agricole (44 producers cultivating 446 hectares of hops in Alsace). It provides us today with all of this essential ingredient for brewing desperadas beers; Tradition, Doreleï, Christmas & 3 Alsatian Hops. This allows us to anchor our production more and more in its region while perpetuating the Alsatian sector..
